Cancer Unit Quiz

1. What gene helps regulate the cell cycle and prevent cancer?




✔ Correct! p53 regulates checkpoints in the cell cycle.

✘ Incorrect. Try again!

2. What increases the likelihood of getting cancer?




✔ Correct! More divisions mean more chances for mutations.

✘ Incorrect. Remember: mutations usually happen during cell division.

3. UV radiation increases cancer risk by causing…




✔ Correct! UV radiation damages DNA, leading to mutations.

✘ Incorrect. Focus on how UV light affects DNA.

4. Which of these is an inherited condition that increases cancer risk?




✔ Correct! LFS is caused by inherited mutations in the p53 gene.

✘ Incorrect. One answer is a genetic mutation disorder.

5. Cancer cells form tumors because they…




✔ Correct! They divide uncontrollably and often clump together.

✘ Incorrect. Look for the behavior of cancer cells.
