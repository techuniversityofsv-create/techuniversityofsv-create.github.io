Arctic Bear Evolution Quiz

PDF Summary

This science unit explores how Arctic bear populations are changing due to climate change and environmental shifts. Students investigate polar bears, brown bears, and black bears to understand evolution, natural selection, adaptation, thermoregulation, hybridization, extinction, and biodiversity.

The lessons explain how shrinking Arctic sea ice affects polar bears by reducing hunting opportunities and increasing competition with brown bears. Students analyze bear interactions, overheating evidence, DNA relationships, glacial cycles, speciation, extinction patterns, and conservation efforts.

The unit also investigates how climate change impacts ecosystems, how species evolve over time, and how humans can help prevent extinction through conservation strategies.
